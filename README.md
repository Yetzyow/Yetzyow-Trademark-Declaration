---

## Declaration  
This document serves as a formal declaration of trademark intent and usage for the brand **Yetzyow**, owned by **Yetzirah Mihael Litzow Díaz**, across the territories of the United Kingdom, the United States of America, and Mexico.  

---

## Areas of Activity  

### 1 - Research  
- General research in physics-mathematics, informatics, cognitive science, and ludology, as well as metaphysics and philosophy.  
- Academic publications, printed publications, and educational initiatives in these fields.  

### 2 - Video Game Development and Technological Innovation  
- Design, programming, and commercialization of video games.  
- Innovation in Virtual Reality (VR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), Computer Graphics (CG), and Information Technology (IT).  
- Technological inventions and patents in VR/AI/IT/CG.  

### 3 - eSports and Non-Competitive Gaming  
- Organization, promotion, and participation in electronic sports (eSports).  
- Community engagement in casual and non-competitive gaming environments.  

### 4 - Gaming Content Creation and Community Participation  
- Production and distribution of gaming-related content (video, audio, streaming, written, printed publications).  
- Active involvement in gaming communities, forums, and events.  

### 5 - Filmer & Composer  
**Filmer**  
- Director, Actor, Screenwriter, and Producer, always portraying the character *Yetzyow* — an eccentric ludologist who falls in love with arrogant women holding multiple doctorates (more than 10).  
- He composes bubblegum rock music for them, which they perceive as eccentric and comical, leading to tragicomic situations of immediate rejection but lingering hope for future possibilities.  
- The films explore themes of comedy, romance, and science fiction, as the character *Yetzyow* is also an inventor with a background in Ludology and Physics.  

**Composer**  
- Focused on bubblegum rock, composing songs and performing them at events and tours.  
- Distribution of musical works through streaming platforms.
